The announcement told that store will be closed in 10 mins and kindly asked customers to finish their purchases.
I left the line to quickly take a look. It was a set of colorized photos telling stories from colonial times from Hong Kong's early days to its golden age.

Another announcement reminded that store will be closed in 5 mins.
I wanted to speed up my pace to briefly explore more chapters of the book. But the photos were so stunning that i couldnt move faster than one by one. I didnt want to miss out anything
Finally the last announcement told that the time is up. I was still at the first pages. I closed the book and got back to the line and purchased the book I came for.
